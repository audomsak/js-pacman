Privacy Policy
==============

PacMan only requires the following permissions:
+ publish_actions

These permissions are necessary to interact with your wall. PacMan asks you before publishing anything on your wall.
PacMan does not send any details concerning yourself on distant servers.

As an opensource project you can have a look to its source code on: https://github.com/dubzzz/js-pacman
Running version for Facebook Canvas is: http://dubien.org/fbpacman/
